###  About Me

> "I work where hardware meets software — debugging signal integrity, chasing timing violations, and making memory subsystems behave under real-world constraints."

I'm an **Electrical Engineer** with **5+ years of experience** building and supporting embedded systems on **ARM Cortex-M** microcontrollers. Currently a **Customer Application Engineer at NXP Semiconductors**, where I do deep hardware/software co-debugging for Tier-1 automotive and industrial customers — resolving 300+ technical escalations a year across the i.MX RT, Kinetis, LPC, and MCX product lines.

My focus areas are **memory subsystems** (SDRAM, NOR Flash, FlexSPI/SEMC), **FreeRTOS**, **MCUBoot**, and **low-power optimization** — the parts of embedded engineering where a few microseconds or a misconfigured memory controller make or break a product.
